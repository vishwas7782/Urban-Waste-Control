About Urban Waste Control System

The Urban Waste Control System was developed to streamline waste management and foster collaboration between citizens and municipalities. Our platform simplifies the process of reporting issues, tracking garbage collection, and ensuring cleanliness in urban areas.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a seamless platform that improves waste collection and management, ensuring that concerns raised by residents are resolved promptly and effectively.

Our Vision

To transform the way waste is managed in cities by fostering active participation from both citizens and municipal bodies, creating sustainable and clean urban environments.
